Falcao invents tearGas

Minega patrick 6:49 AM Technology

Lord Mayor, Erias Lukwago and his comrades should not have expected anything different from what they experienced when they met police on their way to Entebbe Airport to welcome Dr. Besigye. They couldn’t go forward. They ought to have expedcted police to deter them from cruising freely in their cars to Besigye’s residence in Kasangati. FDC party officials shouldn’t have been taken aback by the presence uniformed men in Najjanakumbi where the party headquarters are located; they should have expected this because it has always been like this. The police said that they did all this as a preventive measure against possible unrest caused by Besigye’s supporters. Does it mean Besigye’s supporters are chaotic and they have no sense of peace in society? It was about welcoming their beloved ‘president’ I don’t think they had an agenda of causing any trouble.
